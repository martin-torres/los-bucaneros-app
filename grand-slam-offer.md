## The Tip Comparison — USE IT

> *"A waiter costs you 10–15% per table in tips alone — plus base salary, plus no-shows, plus turnover, plus training, plus errors. Los Bucaneros does everything a waiter does for order-taking — takes the order, sends it to the kitchen, confirms the payment, records the customer — for 5%. And it never calls in sick, never quits, and never writes 'sin cebolla' when the customer said 'con cebolla.'"*

**Critical framing:** "Your waiters do less data entry, more hospitality." You're not selling a replacement — you're selling a tool that makes waiters faster and more valuable.

## The Hormozi Value Equation

```
Value = (Dream Outcome × Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort)
```

| Variable | Score | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Dream Outcome | **10/10** | Stop paying waiters for data entry, own your customer data, never lose an order |
| Likelihood | **8/10** | App exists, works, proven E2E. Only variable is customer adoption time |
| Time Delay | **2/10** | Setup in 20 minutes. Value starts day 1 |
| Effort | **2/10** | Put a QR code on a table. That's it. |

**Score: (10 × 8) ÷ (2 × 2) = 80 ÷ 4 = 20**

Anything above 10 is a Grand Slam offer. We're at **20**.
